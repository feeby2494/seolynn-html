Suspension Setups for 8th Gen Corolla

Currently, my car rides on KYB gr2's and Manzo lowering springs. Manzo's are less than a $100 on ebay and are susposedly made by Megan Racing without factory warrenty. Before installing these springs, my front end has a huge wheel gap, while the back end had only a slight gap. This made the front higher than the back, which made my car look like a clown car.

As you can see, these springs made the front and back more even with each other with less gap between wheel and fender. The ride became more stiff, bouncy, and every bit of road can be felt. This did make it harder to keep up with traffic nearby rail road tracks and speed bumps. You will have to slow down to at least 3rd grear for railroad crossings, if not lower for speed bumps.
